SERVICES

Children

Our children are fragile, they have special needs and demand undivided attention; so does their hair. We hold our children at the top of our minds always, so we created a special children section with dedicated child friendly hair stylists to attend to their hair and other beauty needs. Making their hair with us is like spending time at the playground. They are our precious little jewels and we have children hairstyles and treatments to nurture these young beauties.
